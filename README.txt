                           CONTROLS

KEYBOARD:
  Movement:     W/A/S/D or Arrow Keys
  Interact:     SPACE or Z
  Attack/Punch: X
  Counter:      Y
  Dodge/Hide:   C
  Jump:         SPACE
  Gadget:       Q
  Cycle Gadget: TAB
  Pause:        ESCAPE
  Map:          M

CONTROLLER (PlayStation Layout):
  Movement:     Left Stick or D-Pad
  Interact:     Cross [X]
  Attack:       Square [_]
  Counter:      Triangle [^]
  Dodge:        Circle [O]
  Jump:         Cross [X] / D-Pad Up
  Gadget:       R2 Trigger
  Cycle Gadget: R1
  Pause:        Options/Start
  Map:          Select/Back

================================================================================
                          HOW TO PLAY
================================================================================

KaidoTrainer is an educational game where you explore Agricommune village,
meet NPCs, and learn electronics through building real circuits.

- Explore the farm, cornfield, lakeside, and town center areas
- Talk to villagers to learn about the world and get quests
- Build circuits to progress the story
- Use your gadgets wisely in combat and stampede challenges
- Your AI companion Kaido will help guide you

================================================================================
                         GAME AREAS
================================================================================

1. FARM        - Starting area with grandmother's house and shed
2. CORNFIELD   - Farmer Wen's area with LED chain quest
3. LAKESIDE    - Dock area with fishing NPCs
4. TOWN CENTER - Shop, Town Hall, and Bakery
5. STAMPEDE    - Animal dodging minigame (left from farm)
